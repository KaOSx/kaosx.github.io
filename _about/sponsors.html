On this page you'll find a list of companies and individuals who are making the development of KaOS into something a bit bigger, more public, possible.

A powerful build-server is vital to keep up for a rolling distro. Dezponia, who has a long-standing history of supporting small FOSS projects with exactly that kind of needed hardware, has decided to support KaOS too. No link, just an homage to Dezponia's favorite cartoon.


LayerJet has graciously offered a first mirror for KaOS.

Vultr is what is used for both KaOS servers. It is one of the very few VPS providers that offers complete custom ISO installs. Thus giving the option to have the main server running on KaOS.

Sign-up from this KaOS sponsor link, and you will support KaOS.

Dacentec has graciously offered a second mirror for KaOS.

MerMouY has created a first French and second European mirror for KaOS. This is a first KaOS-user created mirror.
Adam has created a first Czech and fourth European mirror for KaOS sponsored by Connect Plus.

Mirror sponsored by, HostingXtreme, Hosting space and Domain Name provider, Unlimited Hosting plans, Wordpress Hosting, Reseller Hosting.

A mirror in France sponsored by educational association LIGNUx, which encourages the spreading of free and/or open sourced SO, softwares and games. They also post Tutorials and culture related articles.
